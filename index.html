Research Overview

My research aims to enable accurate and efficient human sensing using commodity wireless devices. I explore how channel-state information can be compressed, denoised, and mapped to human pose using deep neural networks.

I am particularly interested in lightweight models that can run on edge devices, as well as cloud-based pipelines that scale to multiple users and environments while preserving privacy.

Selected Projects
