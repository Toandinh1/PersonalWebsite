About Me

Hi! I am Toan. Nice to meet you.

My name is Toan, and you can also call me Brian. I was born and raised in Vietnam, a small country with amazing people.

I am currently a Ph.D. student at Dartmouth College, working under Dist. Prof. Tam Vu on wearable devices for healthcare applications.

Before coming to the United States, I earned a Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from Hanoi University of Science and Technology (HUST), Vietnam. During that time, I was fortunate to work with Dr. Thien Van Luong and Asst. Prof. Van-Dinh Nguyen, who inspired and guided my first steps in academia.
